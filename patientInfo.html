PATIENT INFORMATION

At Nisswa Smiles, you will have a different dental experience. Whether seeking general, cosmetic, or therapeutic dentistry, our mission is to provide high-quality dental care and patient education in a relaxed and welcoming environment. We enjoy getting to know each patient on a personal level. We treat our patients with compassion and honesty. Stop in anytime for a cup of coffee and see the comfortable environment with the latest in technology.

Questions?

Just like every mouth is unique, every question is different. If you have a question, please call us at 218-963-6330. We would love to provide you with personalized answers.

Our Advanced Equipment

From the full-scale autoclave in our lab, which ensures that all of our tools are completely sterilized for the prevention of contamination, to all of our dental tools and our CEREC machine, we are fully equipped with the latest technology in our field. All of our electronic dental tools are quiet, without a lot of vibration for your convenience. You can trust our pain-free process.

The Best Compliment We Can Receive Is Your Referral

Our patients love telling their friends and family about their unique experience with our dental practice, and we hope that you will too!

Anxiety About The Dentist?

We understand that going to the dentist can be stressful, and our team wants to help relieve your anxiety. Our office has a warm and relaxing feel, and our staff is here to comfort you. Let us know what aspects are stressful, and we can cater to your needs to reduce your anxiety. The first step to taking control of your dental care is calling. Together we can set up an appointment for you to come in and talk to our team. We don't need to do anything right away, and we won't pressure you into treatments. We like to provide patients with the information they need to make their own educated decisions about their bodies. We can also provide:

  • * Blankets
  • * TV's to play in the background as a distraction
  • * Nitrous oxide or "Laughing gas"
  • * Feel free to bring in a playlist to listen to and tune out
