Our Services

Nisswa Smiles provides compassionate and cutting-edge dental care for your complete wellness. Whether establishing long term care or seeking a second opinion, offer a wide range of professional services, including cosmetic and therapeutic treatments to meet your needs.

Preventative Care

Teeth Cleaning

Getting your teeth regularly cleaned is more than just a cleaning. We also check for early signs of decay and oral cancer, which is an essential part of good oral and preventative care.

One Visit Crowns and Bridges

Avoid multiple trips to the dentist. With our CEREC technology, we can custom make your crowns and bridges the same day.

Veneers

Thinking about veneers? Veneers can be used for a wide range of issues including chipped or worn teeth. Give us a call and we'll find the right fit for you.

Implants

Implants are a great long term option for those with missing teeth. Restore your smile with dental implants.

Sleep Apnea

For those experiencing obstructive Sleep Apnea, let us make a sleep appliance for you to ensure a great nights rest.

Denture and Denture repair

We provide complete and partial dentures as well as denture repair services for those who need them.
